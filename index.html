Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are some of the procedures that a person goes to in the emergency department?

    The Best Hospital in Jaipur ensures that every treatment and procedure is performed precisely in the emergency department. Some of the treatments and procedures include radiological studies, lab work, ultrasound, CAT scan, EKG for any kind of cardiac review, physicians’ evaluation, and medical administration.

  • Are visitors allowed at Arya Healthcare during the treatment?

    Yes, the number of visitors for any kind of treatment is to be kept to a minimum so that it doesn’t cause any complication for the patient. Usually a number of two visitors per patient is allowed in most of the department and the number may change as per the CBD of the condition.

  • What do I need to bring during my first consultation with the physician?

    When you visit a doctor at our hospital, make sure to bring previous medical reports and records and other relevant information so that the treatment can be provided precisely. Also, you need to provide detailed information about the medicine you take or have any condition to the healthcare provider.

  • If I am not sure about a treatment and need an opinion, then with whom should I consult?

    As a Top Hospital in Jaipur, we make sure that we have a full-fledged team of doctors and experts in each department. If you need an expert or second opinion, then you can consult it with our team.

  • My family doesn’t have any kind of mental illness history. Does that make me immune from such conditions?

    Your family history plays a vital role in developing any kind of condition that is not the only factor when it comes to mental illness. One can develop mental conditions depending on the environment and psychological factors. So, anyone can develop a mental health condition.
